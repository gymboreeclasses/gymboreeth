Welcome to Gymboree Play & Music

Curious minds play at Gymboree Play & Music! Step into a world of color and adventure this May with Mad Hatter tea party events that bring wonderland alive through play. Enjoy story time inspired by Lewis Carroll’s Alice in Wonderland. Ride a caterpillar, spin in a giant tea cup and design a wild hat. Join us for special mother’s day fun too! You’ll also enjoy unlimited Open Gyms and we’ll waive your initiation fee when you enroll.

Programs that inspire creativity, confidence and friendship: Preview a FREE class and discover why we’re the global leader in early childhood learning. We offer expertly designed play, art, music, sports and school readiness classes for children newborn to five years old. Let us help you find a program you and your child will love!

THINGS TO KNOW
Here are a few helpful hints for making your first experience at Gymboree enjoyable!
-Please arrive no more than 5 minutes early.
-Dress your child in comfortable play clothes and gripper socks if your child is walking. Parents must wear socks.
-Let your child lead you. Follow their desires. Stay within arms-length of your child for safety reasons.
-If o­ne of you is ill, please take advantage of our liberal make-up policy.
-No classes: August 30 & 31, September 1 & 25

Our Ocean Township team will be available to offer you and your child a warm and unique experience and provide you with any other information you may need! We look forward to your visit!
