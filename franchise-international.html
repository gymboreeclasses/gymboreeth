Franchise Information

International Master Franchise Opportunity

The Gymboree International Master Franchise offers a long-term business development opportunity to qualified individuals and companies worldwide. As a Master Franchisee you are the Gymboree franchisor for an exclusive international territory and therefore benefit from the following:

  • Generation of revenue by operating sites, selling franchises and receiving royalty payments.
  • Use of Gymboree internationally recognized trademarks.
  • Complete International Master Franchisee training program.
  • Unique equipment package used in our program.
  • Ongoing assistance in marketing, development and operations.
  • Worldwide network of Gymboree Franchisees and Master Franchisees.

Initial Investment

The minimum initial investment starts at approximately US$175,000 and may be considerably higher, depending on the population of the territory and the demographics of the country. This initial investment for a Gymboree Play & Music Master Franchise includes a Master Franchise Fee, pilot center start-up costs and other expenses associated with setting up and operating the Master Franchise organization.

Next Steps

If you are interested in becoming a Master Franchisee and would like to receive additional detailed information, please complete and submit the Request for Franchise Information form. Please note the following steps involved in becoming a Master Franchisee:

  • Complete and submit the Request for Franchise Information form.
  • Gymboree will send you a package on our franchise program.
  • You complete and return the Gymboree franchise application.
  • We invite you to visit our Gymboree headquarters for a private "Discovery Day" meeting.
  • You prepare your business plan.
  • You finalize the Deposit Agreement and complete the Master Franchise Agreement.
  • You attend our Franchise training program.

To begin building a future with Gymboree, or for additional information, please contact us via the Request for Franchise Information form.
