Class Descriptions

Gymboree Play & Music has been fostering creativity and confidence in children ages 0-5 for over 30 years. Today, there are over 600 locations in over 30 countries, making us the global leader in early childhood development programs.

Things to Know

  • Socks: All Adults must wear socks while on the playfloor - no shoes are allowed. Please remember to bring a pair with you.
  • Health: Please bring only healthy children to class. We have a liberal make-up policy so you won't miss out on any of the fun!
  • Hug: Stay within a hug's reach of your child at all times.
  • Refreshment: Food and drink are not allowed on the playfloor.
  • Siblings: For safety reasons and so you and your child can fully enjoy this special time together, siblings should not be brought to class. Ask about our Family Classes!
