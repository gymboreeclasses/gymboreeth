Xavier LOVES Gymboree!

Friday, February 10, 2017 by Gymboree Play & Music Family and Friends

I started bringing my son Xavier to Gymboree Play & Learn classes when he turned 3 months old. He is my 1st born & I wanted to do everything I could to help develop his brain & body. During my sons free trial class he cried so much! He was so stimulated with all the bright colours, other babies, mommies & the teacher! He was so overwhelmed, because prior to this class we were home only with daddy or at grandparents where it was simple & plain. The kind teacher asked if we were coming back & she was so kind & concerned for me because I had said that “he never cries like this!” The teacher reassured me that its all a new environment. After that....we returned almost everyday & my son absolutely loves it there! No more cries, just pure excitement. It was the highlight of our day! He made new baby friends & I made great mommy friends. The teachers are amazing their singing & dancing. Interactions with toys. Bubble time! Parachute time! Gymbo songs! I learned so many songs in class that we sing them at home all the time. My son did his 1st back to tummy flip in class. He learned to sit up, he learned to crawl & stand. Now hes a monster climbing & walking! All the teachers are wonderful there. One teacher that I would like to praise is Catarina, my son was mesmerized by her singing, her sweetness, charm & warmth, he always paid attention to her. You are so lucky to have such an amazing teacher, If there is one thing any new mom would ask me as advice I would tell them to go to Gymboree you will never regret it. The bonding time between mom & baby is so important especially during the 1st year & Gymboree brought that out in us. I highly recommend Gymboree. Love you guys!
